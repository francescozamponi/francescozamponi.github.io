Yielding of amorphous solids

A workshop at ENS Paris, October 26-28, 2017

Organisers: Elisabeth Agoritsas and Francesco Zamponi

Speakers:
  • Jean-Louis Barrat (LIPhy Grenoble) - slides
  • Ludovic Berthier (LCC Montpellier) - slides
  • Edan Lerner (University of Amsterdam) - slides
  • Itamar Procaccia (Weizmann Institute) - slides
  • Pierfrancesco Urbani (IPhT Saclay) - slides
  • Hajime Yoshino (University of Osaka) - slides

This workshop will be focused on the yielding of amorphous materials under load. Related topics such as plasticity, avalanches, and jamming will be discussed.
