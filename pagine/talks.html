    b. Specific to infinite dimensions
    c. More mathematically-oriented
    d. Exact computation of the critical exponents of jamming
  2. Structural glasses:
    a. Dynamically correlated regions and configurational entropy in supercooled liquids
    b. Ginzburg criterion for the dynamical glass transition
    c. Two-level systems in structural glasses
  3. The superglass phase:
    a. A new glassy phase: the superglass
    b. Bose-Einstein condensation in quantum glasses
    c. A review on quantum glasses and the superglass
  4. Quantum cavity method, quantum spin glasses and quantum optimization:
    a. A quantum cavity method
    b. Quantum Spin Glasses on the Bethe lattice
    c. A solvable model of quantum random optimization problems
    d. Transition path sampling for discrete many-body problems
  5. Relation between clustering and algorithmic phase transitions in the random k-XORSAT model and its NP-complete extensions
  6. Fitness landscapes of biological sequences:
    a. Rough fitness landscapes: from protein evolution to protein design
    b. Emergent time scales of epistasis in protein evolution
